title: Real-World SRE
---

A practical guide to Site Reliability Engineering that I wrote for [Packt Publishing](https://www.packtpub.com/). It covers the real-world work of keeping software systems running reliably—monitoring, alerting, incident response, postmortems, safe deployments, capacity planning, and the cultural side of building SRE teams.

The book is aimed at software developers and engineers who are starting to take on reliability work, or who want to understand what SRE actually looks like day-to-day beyond the Google SRE book. It's available on [Bookshop.org](https://bookshop.org/p/books/real-world-sre-nat-welch/7ba67827e4fcabf3?ean=9781788628884&next=t), [Amazon](https://amzn.to/4mRxe3D), O'Reilly, and directly from [realworldsre.com](https://realworldsre.com).
