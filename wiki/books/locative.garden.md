title: locative.garden

A fiction short-story collection I'm working on. The stories are loosely connected by themes of place and location—"locative" as in the grammatical case that marks location, the where of things.

The project is in-progress and written in the open. You can follow along by [subscribing on Kickstarter](https://kck.st/3Ft2AHf) or watching the repository at [icco/locative.garden](https://github.com/icco/locative.garden).
