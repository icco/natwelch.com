I have a few RSS-related projects. [robot.villas](/wiki/projects/robot.villas) is an RSS-to-ActivityPub bridge that posts feed updates to Mastodon. [cacophony](https://github.com/icco/cacophony) is an older project that aggregated URLs from Twitter and exposed them as an RSS feed—a way to turn the link-sharing noise of a social timeline into something actually readable with an RSS reader.

RSS remains one of my favorite ways to follow the web. If you want to follow this site, there's a feed at [natwelch.com/feed.xml](https://natwelch.com/feed.xml).
