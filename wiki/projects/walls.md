[walls.natwelch.com](https://walls.natwelch.com/) is a simple gallery of images from my desktop wallpaper rotation. It's a direct window into what I've been looking at on my screen—photos I've taken, art I've found, and whatever else has made it into the folder.

The source is at [icco/wallpapers](https://github.com/icco/wallpapers). New images go in the folder on my desktop and show up on the site.

I have edited most images by at least cropping and resizing at the very least, some with drastically more than that.
