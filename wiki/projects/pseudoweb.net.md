title: pseudoweb.net
---

pseudoweb.net was my blog before I moved everything to [writing.natwelch.com](https://writing.natwelch.com). It was a Jekyll site with about 800 commits of posts and CSS tweaks over the years.

The old content lives on at [icco/pseudoweb](https://github.com/icco/pseudoweb), and the domain still exists, but new writing goes to the current blog. It's where the habit of blogging got built, and the archives are still readable.
