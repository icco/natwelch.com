---
title: Numbers
---

[icco/numbers](https://github.com/icco/numbers) is a little puzzle based on the idea of [Numbers Stations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Numbers_station). I've always been fascinated with these shortwave radio broadcasts that transmitted sequences of numbers, letters, or tones as encrypted messages, typically for espionage. The actual recipients and message content were never confirmed publicly, which gives them an eerie, mysterious quality.

The project is a small puzzle game that plays on that theme: encoded messages, hidden meaning, the feel of intercepting something not meant for you.
