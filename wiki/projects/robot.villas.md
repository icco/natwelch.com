title: robot.villas

Robot Villas is a service operated for free which scrapes RSS feeds and posts them to Mastodon / ActivityPub. Anyone can add new feeds to subscribe to.

It is available at [robot.villas](https://robot.villas) or your local ActivityPub system. You can add any feeds you want published by editing https://github.com/icco/robot.villas/blob/main/feeds.yml and sending me a PR.
